About Us

Welcome to Sakya International School! As one of Sri Lanka's top educational institutions, we are committed to giving our students the best learning environment possible.

With the goal of equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in the globalized world, our school was established in 1990. To meet the varied needs and interests of our students, we provide a wide range of academic programs and extracurricular activities.

At Sakya, we are dedicated to fostering our students' intellectual curiosity, critical thinking abilities, and ethical values because we believe that education is a lifelong journey. Our team of seasoned professionals works arduously to provide a secure, encouraging, and motivating learning environment for our students.

Thank you for considering Sakya International School as your educational partner. We look forward to welcoming you to our community.
